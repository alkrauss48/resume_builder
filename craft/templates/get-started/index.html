How It Works

Let's build an awesome resume together.

Paper resumes dominate the job searching market, but you can't do much with them. No hiring manager will ever see your Microsoft Word resume and think "Wow, this is really well designed." And you might think, that's not the point of your resume - the point is the content and not how it looks. But really, think about it. If something is well built, we tend to spend more time with it and admire the actual content much more than something that is just thrown together. Still, you're pretty limited with an 8.5 x 11 inch sheet of paper. That's where we come in.

Resume Haus specializes in building and hosting high quality resumes that leverage the true power of the web to show off who you really are. We account for all different types of data you may want to show off, like blocks of text, education, work experience, skills, awards, interests, and literally anything you can think of. If it's on your current resume - we'll make it look beautiful on your Resume Haus resume.

If you haven't seen them yet, check out our example template themes to see which one you like best.

How to Begin

You don't pay a cent until your resume is built. Here's the plan:

  • 1) Complete the form below and upload your resume. Leave any additional information such as your desired theme or your Google Analytics id number.

  • 2) We'll receive your information and will begin building your resume.

  • 3) Once we're complete, we'll email you back with a public URL for your resume to verify everything looks good. If not, we'll hammer out what needs fixing.

  • 4) After everything looks good, we'll link you to a payment page. If we don't receive payment within a week of completion, your resume will be temporarily removed until payment is completed.

  • 5) We're done! You can link your resume to anyone you want to impress.
